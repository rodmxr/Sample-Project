Jordan 1 Low x Travis Scott "Reverse Mocha"

Each low-top Air Jordan 1 will feature a similar design as the first two releases. An oversized backward facing Swoosh featured on the lateral, while a regular Swoosh graces the medial. Signature Travis Scott details will include the “Cactus Jack” logo appearing on the tongue, medial side panel and sockliner.
